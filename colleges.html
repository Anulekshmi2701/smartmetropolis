Best Colleges in Thiruvananthapuram

Mahatma Gandhi College Thiruvananthapuram

Mahatma Gandhi College functions with the objective of providing value added quality education to all, with special emphasis on the economically poor sections of the society, upholding the motto “Sa Vidya Ya Vimukthaye”. It has 13 UG, 10 PG and 7 research programmes in the faculties of basic science, humanities and social sciences with more than 2500 students pursuing their studies in various disciplines.

Mar Ivanios College Nalanchira

Mar Ivanios College was established in 1949 and pursues excellence upholding the far-sighted vision of our revered Founder, Servant of God Archbishop Mar Ivanios.The College is the first Institution in the University of Kerala to receive accreditation from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council in 1999. The College was also the first to be re-assessed and re-accredited with ‘A’ Grade by NAAC in 2004.

Govt Arts College Thiruvanathapuram

Government Arts College Thiruvananthapuram,also known as H.H.The Maharaja's College for Arts, is one of the oldest undergraduate and postgraduate, coeducational college located in Trivandrum, Kerala.It was established in the year 1924. The college is affiliated with Kerala University.Regularly securing Kerala University ranks for various courses is a clear indicator of academic performance.
