Thiruvananthapuram International Airport (TRV)

Established in 1932, Thiruvananthapuram Airport is the first airport in the state of Kerala and the fifth international airport of India. Over the years, the airport has undergone several expansions and upgrades to accommodate increasing passenger traffic and modern aircraft.

As a gateway to Kerala, the airport plays a crucial role in the state's tourism industry, facilitating travel to popular destinations such as Kovalam, Varkala, and the backwaters. It supports the local economy by promoting trade, tourism, and providing employment opportunities.

Thiruvananthapuram KSRTC Bus Stand (Thampanoor Bus Stand)

The KSRTC (Kerala State Road Transport Corporation) bus stand in Thiruvananthapuram, also known as the Thampanoor Bus Stand, is one of the primary transportation hubs in the city

The stand serves as a hub for long-distance buses to various parts of Kerala and neighboring states like Tamil Nadu and Karnataka.Interstate bus services are available to major cities like Bangalore, Chennai, and Coimbatore.

Thiruvananthapuram Central Railway Stationss

Thiruvananthapuram Central Railway Station, also known as Trivandrum Central (Station Code: TVC), is one of the major railway stations in Kerala and a key transport hub in the city of Thiruvananthapuram.The station has multiple platforms to handle a large volume of trains and passengers.

Waiting Rooms: Air-conditioned and non-air-conditioned waiting rooms for different classes of passengers. Restrooms: Well-maintained restroom facilities. Food and Beverages: Restaurants, food stalls, and vending machines offering a variety of food and drinks. Shops: Stalls and shops selling books, magazines, travel essentials, and local products.
